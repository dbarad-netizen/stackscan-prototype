How it works

You didn't approve half the AI tools your team uses. RightStack finds them, prices them, and tells you what to do about each one.

1

Feed it real data

Upload a card/bank statement CSV (what you're charged) and a Google Workspace app report (what your team signed into). Both take 2 minutes to export.

2

Get your AI Stack Report

Real spend per tool, recurring vs. annual charges, shadow AI (signed in but never billed to the company), redundancy flags, and projected renewal dates.

3

Act on the deltas

A monthly review of what changed: new tools detected, unused seats, renewals coming due — each with a keep / cut / switch recommendation.

💳 Card / bank statement

Any CSV export from your card or bank. We find the AI charges, detect monthly vs. annual, and price your stack from what you're actually billed.

📄

Drop a statement CSV here, or click to choose

How to export
Amex/Chase/Brex/Ramp/Mercury → Statements → Download CSV. Needs date, description, and amount columns (auto-detected). Multiple months = better detection.

🔐 Google Workspace app report

Shows every third-party app your team has signed into with company Google accounts — this is where shadow AI hides.

🗂️

Drop the Workspace CSV here, or click to choose

How to export
admin.google.com → Security → API controls → App access control → Download list. Or Reporting → Audit → Token, filter "Authorize", export CSV. Needs an app-name column; user/email and scopes columns improve results.
